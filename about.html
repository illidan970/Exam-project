We offer you:

  • Featuring free Wi-Fi and a seasonal outdoor pool, this hotel is located in the heart of Tashkent's business district. Aqua Park and Uzekspocentre Exhibition Center are a 5-minute walk away.
  • Decorated in red and gold, the rooms at Radisson Hotel come with a flat-screen TV and tea / coffee maker. The private bathroom comes with a hairdryer and slippers.
  • The spacious Brasserie Restaurant serves a variety of international cuisine. The dining terrace is open during the summer months and drinks are served at the poolside bar.
  • Guests can relax in the hotel's sauna or book a massage. Tashkent city center is a 10-minute walk away and the Amir Timur Museum is a 15-minute walk away.
  • A 24-hour shuttle service to Tashkent International Airport can be arranged within a 12-minute drive. Free private parking is available.
  • The couple liked the location - they gave it a rating of 8.4 for a trip for two.
